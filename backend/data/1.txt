Nebula Widgets Inc. - Company Overview

Nebula Widgets Inc. was founded in 1998 by Dr. Aris Thorne. The company's headquarters are located in the city of Aerilon.

The flagship product of Nebula Widgets is the "Quantum Sprocket," a revolutionary component for advanced robotics. This product is known for its market-leading energy efficiency and durability. The Quantum Sprocket is manufactured in their main facility in Sector 7.

The core mission of the company is "To revolutionize terrestrial logistics using quantum-entangled parts."

In 2023, Nebula Widgets announced its ambitious new initiative, "Project Starlight." The goal of Project Starlight is to establish the first fully autonomous lunar data center to process hyperscale AI workloads.

The company's three core values are: Innovation, Precision, and Interstellar-Thinking. The chief competitor to Nebula Widgets is a rival firm known as Cygnus Dynamics.
